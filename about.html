About Us

Who We Are

The JiaoTong and ChiaoTung University Alumni Association at Greater Seattle Area (JTUAA Seattle) is a non-profit organization established to connect alumni from all JiaoTong and ChiaoTung universities living in the Seattle area. We operate as a chapter under the CTUAA (交大美洲校友会).

Our association brings together graduates from various prestigious institutions including:

  • Shanghai Jiao Tong University (上海交通大学)
  • Xi'an Jiaotong University (西安交通大学)
  • Beijing Jiaotong University (北京交通大学)
  • Southwest Jiaotong University (西南交通大学)
  • National Chiao Tung University (国立交通大学)
  • National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (国立台湾科技大学)
  • And other related institutions

Our Mission

JTUAA Seattle is dedicated to:

  • Fostering connections among alumni in the Greater Seattle Area
  • Providing professional networking opportunities
  • Supporting career development and advancement
  • Promoting cultural exchange and heritage
  • Contributing to the local community through service initiatives
  • Assisting new graduates and alumni relocating to the Seattle area

Our History

JTUAA Seattle was founded in 2010 by a small group of passionate alumni who recognized the need for a community that could bring together graduates from JiaoTong and ChiaoTung universities living in the Seattle area. What started as informal gatherings has grown into a vibrant organization with hundreds of members.

Over the years, we have organized numerous events, from professional networking sessions to cultural celebrations, family picnics, and educational workshops. Our community continues to grow as more alumni move to the Seattle area to pursue opportunities in technology, business, academia, and other fields.
