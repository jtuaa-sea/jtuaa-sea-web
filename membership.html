Membership

Join Our Community

Becoming a member of the JiaoTong and ChiaoTung University Alumni Association at Greater Seattle Area connects you with a vibrant community of fellow alumni. Our members enjoy exclusive benefits, networking opportunities, and the chance to contribute to our growing community.

Membership Benefits

Networking

Connect with fellow alumni from various JiaoTong and ChiaoTung universities working in diverse industries across the Seattle area.

Event Discounts

Enjoy discounted or free admission to our regular events, including the annual dinner, cultural celebrations, and professional workshops.

Career Resources

Access exclusive job postings, career development workshops, and mentorship opportunities with experienced professionals.

Mentorship

Participate in our mentorship program, either as a mentor sharing your expertise or as a mentee seeking guidance.

Scholarship Support

Help support our scholarship programs for current students and contribute to educational initiatives.

Global Network

Connect with the broader CTUAA (交大美洲校友会) network and alumni chapters across North America and worldwide.
